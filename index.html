About

Yosemite is located in California's Sierra Nevada mountains which are covered with some of the world's most beautiful scenes of nature. Whether, you want to hike, bike, rock climb, or just soak up the glory, Yosemite has something for everyone. It is truly a great experience to visit this magical land filled with ancient Sequoias, cascading waterfalls, and enormous mountains. Come visit!

History

Yosemite was first inhabited by the Ahwahneechee people for thousands of years until the first settlers arrived in the 1800's during the gold rush. In a conflict with the U.S army the tribe was relocated to a new reservation. After the relocation of the tribe the settlers moved in and eventually the area became a tourist hotspot as we know it today.
